Is there anything I need to do before a brazilian wax?


Yes, please exfoliate two times a week prior and once on waxing day. Also make sure hairs are at least 1/4 of an inch long. You may also take a Tylenol beforehand to help with pain!

Can I wax if pregnant or on my period?


Of course, areas will be more sensitive but it is 100% safe.

Should I trim or tweeze between appointments?


No, the growth cycle is very important to achieve the best results, so please refrain from touching up between appointments.
