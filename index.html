Faqs

Can you wax while pregnant?


YES! You'll just be more sensitive to heat and pain

Can I still get waxed if I'm on my period?


Yes, although you will be more sensitive during this time. We just ask that you come in clean with a fresh new tampon!

Should I trim before my wax or sugar appt?


It's not recommended from us, but if its been more than 6 weeks then, yes. Just not too short!

Is there anything I need to do before a brazilian wax?


Yes, please exfoliate two times a week prior and once on waxing day. Also make sure hairs are at least 1/4 of an inch long. You may also take a Tylenol beforehand to help with pain!

How should I prepare for my first wax?


EXFOLIATE! (we sell awesome products for this) This helps to get rid of excess dead skin cells that can block the hairs from being pulled by the root. Plus it helps your experience to be a lot easier. You can also take any type of pain medication at least 30 mins before!

What forms of payments do you accept?


Pretty much all forms!

Do you recommend sugaring or waxing for my first time?


It's really personal preference
